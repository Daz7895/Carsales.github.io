Welcome to Conlon's Car Sales!

We are a passionate team of car enthusiasts dedicated to providing you with the best selection of quality vehicles at competitive prices.

Our journey began in 2010 when two friends with a shared love for cars decided to start a car store that redefines the car buying experience.

Our Mission: At Conlon's Car Sales, our mission is to make your car buying journey enjoyable and stress-free. We strive to exceed your expectations and ensure your satisfaction with every purchase.

Customer-Centric Approach: Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We listen to your needs and preferences to help you find the perfect car that suits your lifestyle.

Wide Selection of Vehicles: Whether you're looking for a luxury sedan, a sporty SUV, or a reliable family car, we've got you covered. Our showroom boasts a diverse range of vehicles to cater to different tastes.

Quality Assurance: Each vehicle in our inventory undergoes a rigorous inspection to ensure its quality and reliability. You can trust that every car you find here is in excellent condition.

Expert Team: Our team of car experts is dedicated to providing you with honest advice and exceptional service. We are here to guide you through the car buying process with expertise and passion.

Community Involvement: We believe in giving back to the community that supports us. Conlon's Car Sales actively participates in local charities and community events.

We invite you to visit our showroom today and experience the difference at Conlon's Car Sales. Our friendly team is ready to assist you in finding your dream car.
